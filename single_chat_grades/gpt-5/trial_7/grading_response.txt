### **Slingshot Design Response: "The Marksman Pro"**

This design is for a modern, high-performance, over-the-top (OTT) target and small game hunting slingshot, prioritizing accuracy, durability, and user ergonomics.

**I. Frame**
*   **Material:** 6061-T6 Aluminum billet
*   **Grade/Thickness:** 12mm thickness throughout the main body.
*   **Shape:** Symmetrical, ergonomic pistol-grip frame with smooth, rounded edges and a pinky-hole for a secure and repeatable grip. The forks are flat-topped for an OTT (Over-The-Top) band configuration.
*   **Overall Dimensions:** 125mm (height) x 85mm (width at the widest point of the forks).
*   **Fork Gap:** 45mm (inner gap between fork tips). Fork tips are 20mm wide.
*   **Weight:** 180 grams (frame only).
*   **Fabrication Method:** CNC machined from a solid billet, followed by bead blasting for a matte finish and Type II black anodization for surface hardness and corrosion resistance.

**II. Sling & Pouch**
*   **Sling Material:** GZK Green Latex Sheeting, 0.65mm thickness.
*   **Active Length:** 180mm from the fork attachment point to the pouch tie-in (unstretched).
*   **Taper/Thickness:** The bands are cut with a taper of 22mm at the fork end down to 18mm at the pouch end to maximize contraction velocity.
*   **Frame Attachment:** "Wrap-and-tuck" method using 2mm wide amber latex strips to securely fix the bands to the top of the forks in an OTT orientation. The pouch is a durable, single-layer microfiber pouch, 80mm x 20mm.

**III. Projectile**
*   **Material:** Hardened carbon steel ball bearings.
*   **Shape:** Spherical.
*   **Diameter:** 9.5mm (3/8 inch).
*   **Weight/Mass:** 3.52 grams per projectile.

**IV. Human Factor**
*   **Draw Length:** 80 cm, anchored consistently to the user's cheek for aiming reference.
*   **Energy Input:** Estimated draw weight of 8.5 kg (18.7 lbs) at the full 80 cm draw length.
