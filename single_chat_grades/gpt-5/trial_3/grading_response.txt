### **Slingshot Design Response: The "Birchwood Marksman"**

This design is for a modern, ergonomic, high-performance slingshot intended for target shooting. It prioritizes accuracy, comfort, and durability using common, high-quality materials.

**I. Frame**
*   **Material:** Laminated Baltic Birch Plywood.
*   **Grade/Thickness:** 11-ply, 18mm (0.7-inch) total thickness.
*   **Shape:** An ergonomic, asymmetrical "Over-The-Top" (OTT) frame with a distinct palm swell for a right-handed user and a pinky-hole for a secure grip.
*   **Overall Dimensions:** 125mm (Height) x 90mm (Width at forks).
*   **Fork Gap:** 45mm (internal gap between the fork tips).
*   **Weight:** Approximately 150 grams (5.3 oz) after finishing.
*   **Fabrication Method:** CNC routed from a single block of plywood for precision, followed by manual rounding of all edges with sandpaper (120 to 400 grit) and finished with three coats of boiled linseed oil for weather resistance.

**II. Sling & Pouch**
*   **Sling Material:** Theraband Gold latex therapeutic sheeting.
*   **Active Length:** 180mm (18 cm) from fork attachment to pouch tie-in.
*   **Taper/Thickness:** 0.76mm thickness. Bands are cut with a 25mm (at fork) to 20mm (at pouch) taper for optimal energy transfer and velocity.
*   **Frame Attachment:** "Wrap and Tuck" method. The bands are wrapped 4-5 times around the top of the forks and the end is tucked securely underneath the wraps. Thin amber latex strips are used for the final tie-off.

**III. Projectile**
*   **Material:** Grade 100 Carbon Steel Ball Bearing.
*   **Shape:** Sphere.
*   **Diameter:** 9.5mm (3/8 inch).
*   **Weight/Mass:** 3.5 grams.

**IV. Human Factor**
*   **Draw Length:** Designed for a standard adult draw length of 80 cm (31.5 inches), measured from the fork tip to a consistent cheek anchor point.
*   **Energy Input:** Requires approximately 9 kg (~20 lbs) of pull force to reach the full 80 cm draw length.
