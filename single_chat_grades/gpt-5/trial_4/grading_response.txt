### **Slingshot Design: "The Birch Marksman"**

This design is for a classic, ergonomic, over-the-top (OTT) style slingshot intended for target shooting and plinking. The design prioritizes accuracy, comfort, and repeatable performance with medium-power bands.

**I. Frame**
*   **Material:** 12-ply Baltic Birch Plywood.
*   **Grade/Thickness:** 18mm (approx. 3/4 inch), BB/BB Grade.
*   **Shape:** An ergonomic, asymmetrical "pistol grip" style frame designed for a right-handed shooter, with rounded edges and a comfortable palm swell. The forks are flat on top for OTT band attachment.
*   **Overall Dimensions:** 125mm (Height) x 90mm (Width at forks).
*   **Fork Gap:** 45mm internal gap between the fork tips. Each fork tip is 22.5mm wide.
*   **Weight:** Approximately 140 grams (finished frame).
*   **Fabrication Method:** CNC routed from a single plywood blank. Hand-sanded to a 400-grit finish, with three coats of boiled linseed oil for weather resistance.

**II. Sling & Pouch**
*   **Sling Material:** Theraband Gold latex sheeting.
*   **Active Length:** 160mm (the length of the band from fork to pouch when relaxed).
*   **Taper/Thickness:** 0.75mm thick latex, cut with a 22mm (at the fork) to 18mm (at the pouch) taper for efficient energy transfer.
*   **Frame Attachment:** "Wrap and tuck" method, secured with 2mm amber latex tying strips.

**III. Projectile**
*   **Material:** Case-hardened steel ball bearings.
*   **Shape:** Spherical.
*   **Diameter:** 9.5mm (3/8 inch).
*   **Weight/Mass:** 3.5 grams.

**IV. Human Factor**
*   **Draw Length:** Designed for a standard adult draw length of 81cm (32 inches), with an anchor point at the corner of the mouth.
*   **Energy Input:** The bandset provides approximately 18 lbs (80N) of draw force at full extension.
