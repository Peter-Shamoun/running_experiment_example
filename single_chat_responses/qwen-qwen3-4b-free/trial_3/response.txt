### **2. Energy Transfer Analysis**  
#### **A. Elastic Potential Energy Storage**  
- **Formula**:  
  $$
  E = \frac{1}{2} k x^2
  $$  
  - **Variables**:  
    - $ k = 100 \, \text{N/m} $ (limb spring constant).  
    - $ x = 0.5 \, \text{m} $ (max stretch).  
  - **Calculation**:  
    $$
    E = \frac{1}{2} \times 100 \times (0.5)^2 = 12.5 \, \text{J}
    $$  
  - **Efficiency**: 95% (accounting for 5% energy loss in frame flexure).  

#### **B. Kinetic Energy Transfer to Projectile**  
- **Formula**:  
  $$
  KE = \frac{1}{2} m v^2
  $$  
  - **Variables**:  
    - $ m = 0.02 \, \text{kg} $ (projectile mass).  
    - $ v = \sqrt{\frac{2E}{m}} = \sqrt{\frac{2 \times 12.5}{0.02}} = 22.36 \, \text{m/s} $.  
  - **Result**:  
    - **Velocity**: 22.36 m/s (equivalent to a 200-yard throw).  

#### **C. Precision Optimization**  
- **Trajectory Calculation**:  
  - **Range**:  
    $$
    R = \frac{v^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g}
    $$  
    - $ \theta = 45^\circ $ (max range for given velocity).  
    - $ R = \frac{(22.36)^2}{9.81} \approx 50.7 \, \text{m} $.  
  - **Accuracy**:  
    - **Aerodynamic drag**: $ F_d = \frac{1}{2} \rho v^2 C_d A $.
