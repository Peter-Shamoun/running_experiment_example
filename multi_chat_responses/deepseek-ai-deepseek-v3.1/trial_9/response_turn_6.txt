### **3. Force Production & Performance Calculations**

**Step 1: Calculate Spring Constant (k) for a Single Band Layer**
\[ k_{layer} = \frac{E \cdot A}{L_0} \]
*   **E** = Elastic Modulus of Optimized Polymer ≈ **5.0 x 10⁶ Pa**
*   **A** = Cross-sectional Area = **0.0762 m * 0.002 m = 1.524 x 10⁻⁴ m²**
*   **L₀** = **0.3 m**

\[ k_{layer} = \frac{(5.0 \times 10^6 \, \text{Pa}) \cdot (1.524 \times 10^{-4} \, \text{m}^2)}{0.3 \, \text{m}} = 2,540 \, \text{N/m} \]

**Step 2: Calculate Total Spring Constant (k_total)**
\[ k_{side} = 4 \cdot k_{layer} = 10,160 \, \text{N/m} \]
\[ k_{total} = 2 \cdot k_{side} = 20,320 \, \text{N/m} \]

**Step 3: Calculate the Initial Force (F₀) at Full Draw (Δx = 1.2 m)**
\[ F_0 = k_{total} \cdot \Delta x = 20,320 \, \text{N/m} \cdot 1.2 \, \text{m} = 24,384 \, \text{N} \]
**Total Initial Force Output: 24.4 kN** *(This is the force required to hold the system at full draw.)*

**Step 4: Calculate Theoretical Projectile Acceleration & Velocity**
*   **Initial Acceleration (a):** \( a = F_0 / m = 24,384 \, \text{N} / 0.005 \, \text{kg} = 4,876,800 \, \text{m/s}^2 \)
*   **Exit Velocity (v):** \( v = \sqrt{2 \cdot a \cdot \Delta x} = \sqrt{2 \cdot 4,876,800 \cdot 1.2} \approx 2,424 \, \text{m/s} \) **(Hypersonic: Mach ~7.1)**
*   **Theoretical Range (45° launch, no drag):** \( R = v^2 / g = (2,424)^2 / 9.81 \approx 600,000 \, \text{m} \) **(600 km)**
